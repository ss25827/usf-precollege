Learn with STEM ACADEMY
An intensive 7-day residential program that will directly involve students in inquiry, discovery, and exposure to research in STEM disciplines. The 2012 STEM Academy will focus in the area of Diabetes: From Basic Research to Bedside. Students will have access to an internationally recognized faculty and USF's state-of-the-art research facilities.
