Frequently Asked Questions

Are there Scholarships available for Pre-College Programs?

Yes, The Global Sustainability program is taking scholarship applications. Please contact Bessie Skoures 813-974-1256 for more information regarding scholarship opportunities.

What is the deadline to apply?

The deadline to apply is June 1, 2012. Payment must be made in full.

Is the Online application the only part of registering?

After completing registration, all students must sign and turn in the Waiver, Assumption of Risk, Release of Liability form, Health and Accommodation Request Form and the Pre College Code of Conduct. Please review the Pre-College Terms and Conditions Page.

Do all the programs have the same registration process?

No, the Online Music program and the S.T.E.M (Mathematics) program have different registrations processes. Please visit the registration page for more information on how to apply.

Are there discounts for USF Alumni, Faculty or Staff?

Yes! We offer a 10% discount for USF Alumni, Faculty and Staff. Please contact our office 813-974-4926 for the discount code.

What is the refund policy?

Before March 25, 2012 - Full refunds will be granted
March 26 to April 25, 2012 - 75% of payment will be refunded, minus the $75 registration fee
April 26 to May 25, 2012 - 50% of payment will be refunded, minus the $75 registration fee
After May 25, 2012 - No refunds will be granted

Are these programs for-credit?

Online music and S.T.E.M (Mathematics) are the only for-credit programs.

Can you take more than one program?

Yes, as long as the dates don't overlap you may register for multiple programs.

Is food included in the price of the program?

Yes. Commuter programs include lunch, and Residential programs include breakfast, lunch and dinner.

What is the parking policy?

Students who register for Residential programs are not allowed to park on campus. Please make arrangements for drop off and pick up. Students who register for the Commuter programs can park on campus but Drop off is preferred. If you would like to park on campus, please purchase parking permits when you register.

Are all the programs located on the USF Tampa Campus?

No, the Marine Science program will be located on the St. Petersburg campus, but transportation will be provided from the USF Tampa Campus for students who need this service. All other programs will be on the Tampa Campus.

How will transportation be provided for fields trips?

Transportation will be provided to and from all field trips.

Will my child be supervised?

Yes, there will be adult supervision available at all times.

Will students need money?

Students should bring money for snacks, souvenirs and/or other items.

What should students bring with them?

This will vary by program, students will be sent a supply list indicating what to bring once registration has been completed.

What type of accommodation will students be assigned to?

For residential programs students will be staying in single style or double occupancy suites.

USF is an Equal opportunity / Equal Access / Affirmative Action Institution. "To request accommodations for Pre-College students with special needs or disabling conditions contact USF Pre-College (813)-974-4926 to discuss the specific needs of your child."
