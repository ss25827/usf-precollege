FAQ

What are the dates of the courses?
Course dates are June 27, 2012 - August 3, 2012.

What are the requirements and/or prerequisites?
These are introductory music courses. There are no requirements and/or prerequisites needed and students do not need to have a background in music.

Are these courses accredited?
These courses are offered as part of USF's regular credit course curriculum. The University of South Florida us accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.

How many credit hours are the courses?
Each course is 3 credit hours. Students who choose to take all three can earn a total of 9 credits.

Are these credits transferable?
Credits earned for these courses are transferable .

How much will each course cost?
Estimated cost per course: $648.18 (Additional fees and materials may apply).

Are musical instruments, books, or other materials required for the courses?
Musical instruments are not required. Some courses require downloading audio and software. Books are required for certain courses.

Is Financial Aid available for the courses?
Unfortunately financial aid is not available for these courses.
