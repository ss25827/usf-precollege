Pre-College Programs

USF Pre-College offers summer programs for high school and home school teens. With a diverse selection of creative and scientific initiatives, Pre-College students can explore university-level academics within a supervised environment. USF Pre-College is more than summer camp, it's an opportunity to prepare for the college experience, discover potential careers, and learn from expert faculty.
