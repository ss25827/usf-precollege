It's Easy To Register

Register for USF Pre-College programs online; by telephone, fax, or mail; or in person. All online registrations must be paid by credit card. We accept Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express.

Online Registration

Online registration is the easiest and fastest way to enroll in USF's summer programs. Select from the program listing and then click 'Register Now.' Then create a profile and complete your purchase. Have questions or need help? Just call, fax, or email us.

Early Bird Incentive* - Register Early to Save!

For the month of April, USF Pre-College programs have an early bird incentive. Register by April 30, 2012, to take advantage of a $50 discount on program pricing. Registration deadline is June 1, 2012. *Does not apply to STEM Mathematics, STEM Academy or Online Music programs. .

Application and Program Cost

A non-refundable registration fee of $75.00 is included in the program cost **. Payment is due in full at time of registration. Each program is priced differently. Please review each program description carefully and check the final cost before finalizing your order.
** The $75 registration fee does not apply to STEM Mathematics, STEM Academy or Online Music programs.

Refund and Cancellation Policy

March 26 to April 25, 2012 - 75% of payment will be refunded, minus the $75 registration fee
April 26 to May 25, 2012 - 50% of payment will be refunded, minus the $75 registration fee
After May 25, 2012 - No refunds will be granted

USF reserves the right to cancel Pre-College programs. If USF cancels a program, we can transfer you to another program or refund your entire payment, including the $75 registration fee. All refund requests must be received in writing and emailed to precollege@usf.edu.
