USF Pre-College 2012

  • Experience university life
  • Meet friends with similar interests

  • Foster creative thinking and learning

The USF Pre-College 2012 invites high school students from throughout the country and around the world to an outstanding pre-college experience at the University of South Florida. From June to July 2012, students with exceptional ability and promise pursue academic interests, discover career opportunities, earn college credits, and explore the independence of university life. Beyond the classroom, seminars and social activities further the exchange of ideas with equally talented peers.

The USF Pre-College 2012 offers a diverse group of courses that are at the cutting-edge of theory, thought, and technology. Courses are enriched with field trips and guest speakers that take Pre-College Students from theory to practice. Students enroll in one of 13 courses, selecting from a broad range of academic disciplines. University faculty provides innovative instruction in a learning environment that encourages creative problem solving.

A taste of college life

USF Pre-College 2012 students will enjoy the beautiful, tree-lined USF campus and its outstanding facilities, meet new people and make lifelong friends. Students come from all across the United States and the world. Students live together in a dorm on campus (*residential programs only) or within commuting distance with their families and participate in a variety of programs, activities, and excursions designed to prepare them for college life.

College-level academics with expert faculty

USF is ranked 8th as an 'Up-and-Comer' among the country's national universities in the 2012 edition of U.S. News & World Report's Best Colleges edition. USF is included in three lists in the exclusive rankings of more than 1,600 schools. USF ranks in 'Best National Universities,' 'Up-and-Comers,' and 'A-Plus Schools for B Students' lists.

Live on a vibrant college campus

Live away from home and experience the independence and responsibility of college life in a safe, secure, and supervised environment. Pre-College students live together in an air-conditioned residence hall on the main USF campus (unless they live with their family within commuting distance of the university). USF residence life staff helps students make the most of their summer academic experience.

Take more than one course

Many accepted students choose to attend multiple sessions throughout the summer, stringing together a series of one or two week courses. This option offers students the opportunity to create their own course of study and immerse themselves in a variety of subjects all summer long.

A USF Pre-College 2012 experience goes far beyond the classroom.

Outside of class, you will make friends, attend events and social activities on campus, and have time for summer fun. Preparing for college continues with USF Pre-College 2012 extensive Co-Curricular activity calendar -- Workshops and presentations designed specifically to help you succeed with your transition to college-level academics.
